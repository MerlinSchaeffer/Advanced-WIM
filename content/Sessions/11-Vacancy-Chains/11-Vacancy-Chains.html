Readings: Chase (1991).

Presentation: Brüderl, Preisendörfer, and Ziegler (1993) and Abbott (1990).

Lecture slides: Lecture 11

How much are individual’s career determined by an overall chain of vacancies that runs through organizations? And can this idea be applied to other phenomena and topics? In this session we will talk about a theory of opportunity structures for people to get ahead or to get stuck.

References

Abbott, Andrew. 1990. “Vacancy Models for Historical Data.” Social Mobility and Social Structure 81–102.
Brüderl, Josef, Von Peter Preisendörfer, and Rolf Ziegler. 1993. “Upward Mobility in Organizations: The Effects of Hierarchy and Opportunity Structure.” European Sociological Review 9(2):173–88. doi: 10.1093/oxfordjournals.esr.a036670.
Chase, Ivan D. 1991. “Vacancy Chains.” Annual Review of Sociology 17(1):133–54. doi: 10.1146/annurev.so.17.080191.001025.
