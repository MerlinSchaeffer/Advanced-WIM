Lecture slides: Lecture 5

The Danish government does a lot to fight parallel societies. But what does sociology say about that? As we will discuss, both classic and recent sociological scholarship sees many benefits in so-called ethno-religious enclaves. In many regards, they can actually suport the integration of immigrants and their children.
